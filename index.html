NFT Secondary Market

Abstract

On the brink of Metaverse being fully expressed into reality, Importance of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFT) has emerged though different domains like Digital Arts, Music Industry and Gaming. Though advanced platforms for the creation and primary transactions of NFTs are available, a proper secondary markets to liquify the created NFTs are lagging behind. Therefore RIMELabs is dedicated to developing a proper secondary financial market presenting three major functionalities for NFTs, Buying and Selling of NFTs, Rental of NFTs and Mortgage Functions using NFTs. The development of the AvianFinance Secondary Market will be based on the Avalanche Platform due its promising platform architecture and the state of the art consensus engine. We will capture design inspirations from similar development efforts available in the Ethereum Blockchain to improve and benchmark our effort. Finally the new framework will be containerized to a fully functioning platform covering the full-stack, where users can create, sell, buy, engage in renting mechanisms or mortgage functions successfully using their NFTs.

Buying and Selling

Buying and selling of NFTs is a primary requirement in the market and the AvianFinance Platform supports three unique buying and selling mechanisms.

  • Fixed Price

    Fixed Price mechanism allows the seller to define a price in a suitable currency and a buyer willing to match the price can obtain the ownership. AvianFinance Platform provides settlement trust for the transactions as well.

  • Auctions

    Auctions allows the seller to define a base price for the NFT and a duration for the auction to happen. At the auction time, potential buyers need to add bids to the NFT and at the end of the auction period, the bidder with the highest bid will attain the ownership. AvianFinance Platform provides settlement trust for the transactions as well.

  • Offers

    Offers allow potential buyers to send buy offers for NFTs not listed for sale and also allow buyers to send sell offers with a lower price to Fixed Priced listed NFT.

Rental Function

Besides buying and selling NFTs open up several options to generate a passive income through NFT renting. Renting provides the benefits of having an NFT without the financial commitment of investing in it. It has potential benefits for both NFT lenders and borrowers. For the borrower, it opens up the opportunity to join the NFT community and take advantage of a digital asset that they wouldn’t be able to afford. For the lender, it creates a financial incentive by allowing them to earn money on NFTs that might not be utilized for a certain period of time. There are several rental use cases that are possible in the current digital market.

  • Gaming assets
  • Event tickets
  • Virtual land
  • Subscription
  • Temporary Assets

Through this project, we propose a novel architecture for the NFT rental protocol. There are two broad ways where NFTs can be rented out which are collateral-free solutions - Upfront rental and Reward share.

Upfront Rentals

In the upfront rental mechanism, the NFT owner(lender) decides the daily rental price and the maximum rental duration when putting it up for rental. When the renter requests to rent by defining the rental duration and paying the rental fees, the smart contract assigns the user right to the renter. Then the renter receives access to the utility of the NFT. When the rental period ends, the NFT is automatically transferred back to the owner through the rental platform. Games and events can take advantage of this solution to enable rentals on their platforms without users risking their NFTs getting defaulted

Reward Share

In the reward share mechanism, four entities work together, the NFT owner, the NFT renter, Gaming Platform and the AvianFinance Platform. Gaming platform provides a list of available assets which can be rented (which were listed by renters in AvianFinance Platform), the renter decides which asset to rent and asks the gaming platform to do the necessary transactions. Then the gaming platform requests the AvianFinace Platform to transfer the user role of the corresponding NFT to the renter and as payment, the rewards the renter receives by using the asset is splitted between the renter and the owner. Collateral is not not required as the user role will be automatically assigned to the owner after the defined rental period. This function will be beneficial to all the parties involved, making stagnant NFTs highly usable and liquid.

Mortgage Function

Generally a mortgage is a loan taken to buy a digital or physical asset by using the same asset as a collateral. The due loan amount is required to be paid in instalments including the interest, as agreed at the time of signing the contract. The AvianFinance Market provides two mortgage solutions, Obtaining a Loan to buy an asset, keeping the same asset as collateral. Obtaining a Loan to buy an asset, keeping a different asset as collateral. Neither of these functions have standardised solutions in the NFT world and RIMElabs intend to publish a standard specifying the underlying architecture of proposed Mortgage solutions.
